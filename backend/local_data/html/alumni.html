Get Connected, Stay Connected

As a Portland State University graduate, you are part of the worldwide network of more than 210,000 alumni.

Every day, alumni like you are building a better future—and together, we’re building a stronger community.  

No matter where you live, you can stay connected, get involved, and inspire the next generation of Vikings:

Give

Your gift to support PSU can make a lasting impact. Your contributions can help future students, fund programs you care about, and drive initiatives across campus. Invest in PSU’s future and ensure excellence for generations to come.

Advocate

Join PSU Advocates and share the importance of higher education and Portland State  University. As a volunteer, you can connect with Oregon lawmakers, attend events and help advance the goals to meet the needs of an outstanding teaching and research institution in Portland.

Volunteer

Volunteering with the PSU Alumni Association is a rewarding way to give back. Your time and expertise can make a difference at events like Serve the City Day, as a mentor on PSU Works, or through partnerships that enhance PSU and Portland. Join us today!
