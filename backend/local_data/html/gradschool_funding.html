Funding and Awards

Funding and Awards

Find funding for graduate school

Portland State is committed to providing graduate students with an excellent education at an affordable cost. The cost of graduate school includes tuition and fees (some programs charge a differential tuition). Assess the total cost of your graduate program.

Feeling overwhelmed? Portland State's Financial Wellness Center offers one-on-one appointments with Financial Coaches and is also available to consult over the phone.
