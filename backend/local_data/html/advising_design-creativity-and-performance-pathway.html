Design, Creativity & Performance Pathway

Contact Information:

Phone: 503-725-3822
Email: arts.advising@pdx.edu
Location: 360 Fariborz Maseeh Hall

Majors in this Pathway:

Architecture; Art and Design; Art History; Art Practice; Cultural History of the Arts; Graphic Design; Film; Music; Social Justice & the Arts; Sonic Arts & Music Production; Theater Arts

How to meet with a DCP Advisor:

Students in all majors in this Pathway, including Exploratory/Undeclared, are assigned an advisor based on the last two digits of their PSU ID number. If you would like to request a different advisor than the one assigned to you, please fill out an Advisor Change Request form. If you change your major to a different pathway, your new advisor will be updated automatically within a week.

Use the links below to schedule a phone, virtual or in-person appointment. You will be required to sign in with your Odin account. If you cannot access the appointment scheduler, call our front desk at (503) 725-3822. For in-person appointments, please review PSU's current Mask Policy. Please note that if you have a pre-professional program, you should meet with that advisor, regardless of your major.

Students starting at PSU in a future term: You may meet with a DCP advisor prior to your first term, but must participate in New Student Orientation in order to register for your first term classes. Transfer students should also check out our Transfer Guides!

Faculty Advising

Juniors and seniors admitted to the programs in this pathway can also meet with a faculty advisor. Links to their pages are below:
