PSU Help Center

Welcome to the PSU Help Center

Use the Search bar above or individual Portals below to find support resources and request forms for teaching, learning, and technology services. You may also connect directly with the OIT Help Desk or OAI Teaching Support.

Portals

  1. Public Support Desk

    Anyone may request support for Odin, Duo, and other public services.

  2. IT Service Desk

    PSU employees may request support for technology services.

  3. PSU Support Desk

    PSU students and employees may request support for technology and other campus services.

  4. Faculty Support Desk

    PSU faculty and teaching staff may request teaching support.

Suggested forms

  • Get General IT Support in PSU Support Desk

    Faculty, staff, and students may get support with general IT problems and questions.

  • Software Install in IT Service Desk

    Request software to be installed on desktop or laptop.

  • Get Support with Teaching Technology in Faculty Support Desk

    Faculty and teaching staff may request support with teaching technologies.

  • Hardware Support in IT Service Desk

    Get help with a computer or other hardware.
