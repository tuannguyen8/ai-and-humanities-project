ONLY IN HONORS 

In the Portland State University Honors College, you can dig deep into the urban issues that matter most, join a tight-knit community of highly motivated students, and experience an exceptional education unlike any other. 

As an Honors student at PSU, you have all the benefits of a small liberal arts college embedded in Oregonâ€™s only public urban research university. Our unique focus on the city of Portland opens unmatched opportunities for research, creativity, and academic exploration.

You can work closely with world-class researchers, study abroad with Honors faculty in London or Kenya, and intern with top employers. It all culminates in your senior year, when you not only research, create, and write, but also publish your Honors thesis. 

We welcome students from all academic majors and diverse ethnic, social and economic backgrounds, including many transfer students.

Discover an academically fulfilling curriculum through the lens of our culturally rich, ever-evolving city. And start a conversation with the future.

Celebrating our Honors Graduates

The Honors College Commencement will be Thursday, June 12, 2025 from 2:30pm - 4:30pm in the SMSU Ballroom, room 355.  Doors will open at 2pm and the ceremony will begin at 2:30pm.  Your family and friends are invited and no tickets are necessary.  The ceremony will be 90 minutes followed by a reception in the same location.  Learn more about Honors Commencement.
