Commercial Space Leasing Opportunities

Located in South Downtown, the Portland State University campus presents a unique commercial leasing opportunity for both retailers and business offices. Whether your business seeks a new location that is easily accessible to current employees, or to develop and grow a reliable customer base, the PSU campus offers an ideal business locale in the economic and cultural epicenter of Portland.

To learn about spaces we have available for lease, contact Kimberly Maguire at kmaguire@pdx.edu or 971-563-2199.
