Course Materials

You can find required course materials in the Class Details pane the course listing. 

When browsing the class schedule in the myPSU Registration Hub, click on the Course Title hyperlink.

This will open the Class Details window. Select the Bookstore Links option from the menu on the left.

The Bookstore link on this page will take you to specific materials for this course at the online bookstore.

No Cost and Low Cost Course Materials

To help reduce costs to students many instructors have selected Open Educational Resources (OERs) and other no and low cost options for their course materials. To search for courses using no or low cost materials, select the No Cost Text Materials and/or the Low Cost Text Materials attributes in the Advanced Search Option when searching for classes.
