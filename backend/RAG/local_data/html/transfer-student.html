Cost, Aid, and Deadlines

TRANSFERS FINISH FREE

The Transfers Finish Free program allows eligible Oregon residents to finish their degrees at PSU and pay no standard tuition or fees. You will be automatically considered for Transfers Finish Free when you apply to PSU.

Transfer Program Details about this content

OVERVIEW OF COSTS

PSU puts affordability in action, so you can graduate with more money for your future. To help you pay for college, PSU offers free and discounted tuition for eligible students through Transfers Finish Free, the Western Undergraduate Exchange (WUE), and the Out-of-State Opportunity Scholarship.  There are student employment opportunities on campus as well.

Registration and Advising

All incoming undergraduate students are required to complete New Student Orientation. Completion of Orientation will provide you with course registration access for your first term and you will learn how to connect with your academic advisor. Do not set up an appointment with your academic advisor prior to completing Orientation. We also offer drop-in registration labs where you connect with an advisor and Orientation leaders to answer questions and help guide you through the registration process.

HOW THE PSU CORE CURRICULUM WORKS

Most students will complete either a Bachelor of Arts (BA) or a Bachelor of Science (BS), each of which has specific requirements associated with the degree type. The university's general education curriculum is designed to assure that its graduates have a breadth of coursework from across the university's curricula. At PSU, University Studies and University Honors College are the two options for general education.

University Writing Requirement: Students must complete 2 college-level composition courses or their approved equivalents for their baccalaureate degree requirements.
