Our Career Resources for Diverse and Intersectional Identities 

Our faculty and staff provide unique resources and services to meet the career development and job search needs of our diverse student & alumni populations.

Below are materials that have been developed to support specific identity-based populations:

  • LGBTQ Students and Alumni
  • Students and Alumni of Color
  • International Students and Alumni
  • Students and Alumni With Disabilities
  • Student Veterans


The university also offers a variety of other resources and services that may of interest to diverse students. For more information, see the various Student Support Services.

Want to meet with a Career Professional?

You can find a lot of resources here on our site to help you explore careers and search for jobs/internships. If you would like to talk about this with someone, you are welcome to set up an appointment with one of our team! We also offer workshops to help you choose a major or career, answer questions about internships, prepare you for interviewing, perfect your resume and cover letter, and more!
