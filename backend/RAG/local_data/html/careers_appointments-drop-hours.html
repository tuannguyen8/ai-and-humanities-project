Appointments & Drop-ins

Appointments

We offer 50-minute virtual, phone, and in-person appointments to all current PSU students and alumni (i.e., those who have graduated from PSU or have earned 60+ credits from PSU). You can meet with us to:

  • Identify your values, interests, and skills
  • Explore career options
  • Discuss your job or internship search
  • Create a career plan and set professional goals
  • Navigate career transitions
  • Learn and practice interview strategies
  • Review professional documents like résumés and cover letters
  • Explore graduate programs and other continuing education opportunities 

Drop-in Hours

  • 30-minute drop-in sessions
  • Monday-Thursday (2-3pm)
  • First come, first served

There is limited availability for drop-ins, and there’s a chance that we won’t be able to serve everyone. Due to the brief nature of drop-ins, we encourage you to prioritize what you’d like to discuss with us. If you require more in-depth assistance, please schedule a 50-minute appointment with one of our career counselors.  

Virtual drop-ins: Drop-ins are available via Zoom from 2-3pm, Monday-Thursday. Sign-ups for virtual drop-in sessions open at 9am each day and operate on a first-come, first-served basis. If your desired drop-in slot isn’t available, we encourage you to check the following morning at or after 9am for new availability. Use this link to sign up for a virtual drop-in session.

In-person drop-ins: Drop-ins are available at the University Career Center from 2-3pm, Monday-Thursday, on the third floor of Fariborz Maseeh Hall (342 FMH). Please call us at (503) 725-4613 to ensure that we have availability before your visit.
