International Student Mentor Program

International Student Mentor Program Overview

PSU offers a unique pre-arrival connection with a peer for all new international students. Our mentoring program is an integral part of our commitment to support international students before they arrive and during their adjustment to PSU.

Program Goals
  • Reach out to incoming international students that have been admitted to Portland State University to provide information and assistance with questions or concerns.
  •  Assist new international students in the transition from their respective countries to our university.
  • Enhance the international student experience at Portland State University through personal interaction in an atmosphere of mutual trust and respect.

We are seeking mentors to assist newly admitted international students' transition to life in Portland and PSU. Applicants can be from any cultural background, department, and all education levels. View the full position description for the 2025 - 2026 academic year.

Criteria for Becoming a Mentor Leader

Mentor Leaders will assist with training as well as take on specific leadership roles for the 2025 - 2026 academic year in addition to the regular requirements of the ISMP. This is a great opportunity for returning mentors to gain new experience and develop valuable presentation and planning skills.
