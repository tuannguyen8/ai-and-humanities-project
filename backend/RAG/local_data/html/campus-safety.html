Safer Together

Welcome to Portland State University’s Campus Public Safety Office! 

We are here to ensure PSU is a safe, respectful, positive environment for you to learn, live, work and achieve your goals. We are available 24/7 to support you because your safety matters to us. 

We believe we are all safer when we work together.

Meet Chief Halliburton

Willie Halliburton, PSU’s Chief of Campus Public Safety, believes deeply that safety comes from developing relationships in the community and treating people with respect. His ultimate goal is to build trust with the PSU community. 

Read about Willie Halliburton

Safety in the city

Our urban campus in the heart of Portland is one of the best parts of PSU, offering easy access to internships and hands-on learning. Yet we are our own community with extra security measures to ensure PSU is safe. We invite you to visit and see for yourself!
