Wellness & Health Action Team

Who We Are:

The Wellness and Health Action Team (WHAT) is a team of peer health educators in the Health Promotion department at the Center for Student Health and Counseling (SHAC).

WHAT's goals are to:
  • Inform the Portland State community about relevant health topics and available resources
  • Develop engaging programs on campus to promote healthy decision-making
  • Empower students to utilize accessible strategies to achieve long-term health
  • Advocate for change in campus policies that facilitate student wellbeing and success
     

 

What We Do

  • Campus-wide health promotion events
  • Health education workshops / presentations
  • Event appearances and tabling
  • Department collaborations
  • The WHAT Hut
  • WHAT's Up: A Wellness Podcast
  • Education and resource-sharing on Instagram

How to Contact Us

Please reach out to us by emailing WHAT@pdx.edu if you have any questions or if you want to partner with us.

Sleep Hygiene

With the demanding forces of academics, work, and family life, sleep is often put on the back burner of students' routines. This workshop will help assist students in developing sleep hygiene skills by learning to boost our productivity through proper rest. 

Request this workshop

Productivity & Wellness

This workshop explores how to prioritize both self-care and getting stuff done. Creating better balance can help prevent burnout and integrate more personal wellbeing into the busy life of a college student.

Request This Workshop

Reframing Fitness

This workshop will touch on the perceptions and approaches of the exercise and fitness industry, how to integrate accessible movement into your life, and how to develop and maintain a successful plan

Nutrition

Nutrition goes beyond what food is on your plate; it also has to take into consideration things like access, barriers, mindfulness, and culture.

Life Transitions

In this workshop, we will go over how to integrate a new life change into your life. This workshop will give you the skills to handle the stress that comes with major life changes as well as learning about resilience and positive mental attitude.

Sexual Health
