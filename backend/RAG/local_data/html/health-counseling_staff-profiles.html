Meet the Health Services Team

Available services include routine adult medical care, same day care, lab and x-rays, immunizations, screenings for STIs/STDs, birth control, emergency contraception, wellness exams - pap smears, specialist referrals , exams and support for sexual assault survivors (SANE), acupuncture, student travel appointments, trans health and prescription refills. Learn more about Health Services.

Meet the Dental Services Team

Learn more about Dental Services. Any PSU student taking at least one credit hour per term is eligible to use SHAC Dental Services on a fee-for-service basis. Services are 30 - 50% lower cost than other community dentists and all treatment is done by licensed, experienced providers with your comfort in mind.
