About Politics and Global Affairs

The Department of Politics and Global Affairs is dedicated to understanding the nature of power and governance, and to addressing global concerns such as social justice, human rights, war and peace, development, environmental protection, inequality, health, labor rights and poverty alleviation. We offer undergraduate and graduate programs in both Political Science and International and Global Studies.

All our programs emphasize experiential learning, internships, and interdisciplinary collaboration, and seek to foster innovation and societal impact through research, teaching, and community engagement. 

Our Mission and Values

We are dedicated to the principles of academic freedom, intellectual honesty, and professional excellence. We expect our faculty, staff, and students to reflect the following values:

  • Excellence in Research and Teaching
  • Contribution to Public Good
  • Service to Community
  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusivity
