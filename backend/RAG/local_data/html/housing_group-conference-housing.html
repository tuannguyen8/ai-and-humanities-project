Conference Group Housing

Location & Contact

Broadway Hall
1977 SW 6th Ave., Ste. 210
Portland, OR 97201

shc@pdx.edu
503-725-4336

Availability

Summer 2025: June 20 - September 5.

For questions, or to schedule a virtual or in-person appointment and tour, please email shc@pdx.edu.

Broadway Hall and Ondine Hall will house all of our summer guests. Our conference units are furnished studios. Each unit is furnished with 2 beds (XL twin), desks, chairs, and dressers. All of our units have private bathrooms. Each building features cooling systems in the hallways and common areas. Individual rooms are not air-conditioned, however they do come equipped with fans..

Our buildings are ADA accessible and we also have ADA units available.  Our ADA units have roll-in showers, movable shower handles, and shower benches.  If any guest requires accommodations, please reach out to us.

For more information, please contact us at shc@pdx.edu.
