High School Programs

Custom programs for High Schools:

These programs typically last 1-3 weeks and can include dorm stay, homestay, or staying at a hotel. These programs usually fit one of four standard types:

Typical Custom Programs

  1. English Language & American Culture Programs 
    • Morning English Communication and American Culture classes
    • Afternoon cultural / fun activities 
  2. English Language & Focus Topic Programs
    • Morning English Communication class
    • Afternoon special "Focus" topic class with related excursions / activities
  3. Focus Topic Programs
    • All classes and activities are related to a single theme and may or may not include English language instruction as needed
  4. Visits / Excursions only Programs
    • No English classes but rather topic specific lectures and site visits
    • Cultural and fun activities to explore Portland

All types of High School Programs:

  • Can be 2-4 weeks long
  • Could include homestays, campus dormitories or hotel stay for the housing arrangements

Potential Focus Topics (We are always happy to consider new topics as well!):

*Please click on the Program Name links for sample calendars. 

  • Introduction to U.S. Higher Education / "Bridge" programs
  • Paragraph Writing & Pronunciation OR English Conversation (Mixed level group)
  • UN SDG Goals (Current offerings include mixed topics from: SDG #1: No Poverty, SDG #2, Zero Hunger, SDG #11: Sustainable Cities & Communities, SDG #12 Responsible Consumption & Production., SDG #5 Racial and Gender Inequality, LGBTQIA+ rights, etc.) 
  • Service Learning & Volunteering

Planning a custom program checklist:

  1. We ask for at least 6 months notice to develop a new program in order to provide your group with an excellent experience. 
  2. We recommend a 15 student minimum for new programs. Programs with less than 15 participants are cost prohibitive for partners.
  3. Please answer this short questionnaire to request a quote or a consultation. 
  4. Preferred dates or times of year and any special request for accommodation type. 

PROGRAM INQUIRY FORM

If you would like to request more information about custom programs, please fill out this Program Inquiry form. 

Example Activities

MULTNOMAH FALLS

LARGEST WATERFALL IN OREGON & MOST POPULAR PLACE TO VISIT

Multnomah Falls is located in the famous Columbia River Gorge, which separates Oregon from the state of Washington to the north. 

CONVERSATION PARTNERS

CONVERSATION PARTNER SESSION

Most days students will meet with a Conversation Partner and a small group of other students in order to practice English in a more casual environment! 

OREGON FOOD BANK VOLUNTEERING

SDG #2 NO HUNGER

Students volunteering at the Oregon Food Bank, sorting food and packing boxes for hungry families as part of learning about SDG #2, No Hunger. 

Students Visiting Cannon Beach

Haystack Rock is another of Oregon's famous destinations

Students on our summer programs visit the beautiful Oregon coast, including local attractions such as Haystack Rock or the TIllamook Cheese Factory. 

Woodburn Outlet Mall Shopping

Oregon shopping is tax-free!

Another popular draw of Oregon and our programs is the fact that Oregon has no sales tax. All shopping, dining, and entertainment in Oregon is cheaper than in other states!
