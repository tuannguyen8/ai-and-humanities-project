Patient Portal

The Patient Portal is a secure portal allowing students to communicate with Health Services.

Common Student Expenses

A Student Health Fee covers most Medical and Counseling Services at SHAC. However, there are some services that incur an additional cost. For students with private insurance (i.e. are not on the PSU Student Health Insurance Plan) these charges are billed to the student’s PSU account.

SHAC partners with TeamHealth, a 24/7 external nurse advice line that can help answer your medical questions and/or get you connected with resources when SHAC is closed.  For after hours assistance, contact 1.844.224.3145.

Health Services Provided

Routine adult medical care, same day care, lab and x-rays, immunizations, screenings for STIs/STDs, birth control, medication abortion, emergency contraception, wellness exams, specialist referrals , exams and support for sexual assault survivors (SANE), acupuncture, student travel appointments, trans health and prescription refills.

Off Campus Vision Resources

SHAC does not provide vision services. You can find a variety of options in the surrounding community. Visualeyes located just blocks from PSU, offers a PSU student discount.

Telehealth Services

Telehealth visits use video cameras on your computer or phone to connect you to your provider, meaning you don't have to travel to get the care you need. You will be able to see, hear, and talk to your provider through video-conferencing.  Learn more about the types of health services visits that can be seen via telehealth.

Sexual Health Offerings

There is a wide variety of services available at SHAC including: STI screenings, rapid HIV testing, pregnancy testing, birth control, Sexual Assault Nurse Examination Assessment, emergency contraception, condoms including latex-free and non-lubricated, dental dams, and lubricants.

Want free condoms? The Oregon Health Authority can send you some by mail, visit their website for more information.

Acupuncture at SHAC

Limited appointments per term. Individual sessions (50 min) - Students on the PSU PacificSource Student Health Insurance Plan are covered at 100% (no out-of-pocket cost) at SHAC. For all other insurances - Cost $150. Appointments have in-depth review to discuss individual plans. All acupuncture fees are billed directly to a student's PSU account. Acupuncture has a $30 late cancellation or no-show fee.

To learn more about acupuncture and commonly asked questions, visit the SHAC Acupuncture webpage.

Questions or want to schedule? Contact SHAC at 503.725.2800 or visit us at 1880 SW 6th Ave. Suite UCB 200.

Sexual Assault Resources

Sexual Assault Nurse Examiner (SANE) Program
Supporting students survivors of sexual misconduct:

  • Visits are confidential and a police report does not have to be made to receive an exam (unless you are under the age of 18).
  • Most services are free or include a minimal charge.
  • If there is a potentially  life threatening injury we will assist getting you to an emergency room.
  • Forensic evidence can be collected within 120 hours or 5 days of an assault. If you choose to not report, forensic evidence will be collected anonymously and held for 60 years. You may choose to report in that time.
  • The SANE program works with PSU campus advocates who are available to support you through the SANE process and ensure your access to support services on and off campus.
  • If assault occurred over 5 days ago, you can still access medical services related to assault at SHAC. 
  • A nurse can also  assist in scheduling an appointment with Counseling Services if the patient desires.
  • If SHAC is closed, see “Off Campus” resources below.

Off-Campus Resources

Outside of SHAC business hours, services can be accessed at Portland area hospitals. For more information, contact the 24-hour hotline at Call to Safety 503-235-5333.  Call to Safety resources connect with Call to Safety’s 24/7 crisis line, in-person medical advocacy, follow-up advocacy and support groups.

The PSU Audiology Clinic*

The PSU Audiology Clinic is located on the fourth floor of the University Center Building within the Speech & Hearing Science Department.  We are a fee-free clinic and do not work with or bill any insurance companies. We currently provide diagnostic hearing evaluations to adult patients age 18 and up. Our services include the following: 

  • Otoscopy
  • Standard and extended high frequency audiometry, including air conduction and bone conduction testing
  • Distortion product otoacoustic emissions (DPOAEs)
  • Acoustic reflex and decay testing
  • Word recognition testing
  • Speech-in-noise testing

Interested in making an appointment?  Email audiology@pdx.edu or call (503) 725-2292.

*This service is not offered at SHAC but is available to PSU students.
