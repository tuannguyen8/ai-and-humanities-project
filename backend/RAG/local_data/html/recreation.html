YOU BELONG AT CAMPUS REC

Welcome to Portland State Campus Rec! Portland State's beautiful, modern Rec Center is located in the heart of campus on the Urban Plaza. The Rec Center is on floors 2-4 of the Academic & Student Recreation Center (ASRC). The Outdoor Program office and Equipment Rental Center are just outside and around the corner from our main Rec Center facility, on the corner of SW 5th Avenue and Harrison Street.

Campus Rec members and guests can drop in during Rec Center hours to enjoy our facilities and programs, which are universally designed to provide opportunities for everyone to accomplish their health and wellbeing goals. Our trained staff are here to provide you with assistance and support.
