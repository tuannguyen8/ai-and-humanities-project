About Us

Portland State University’s Summer Housing and Conferences program offers affordable and convenient housing options in Downtown Portland! We serve summer conference groups, summer camps, interns, and visiting students. Come stay with us and enjoy summertime in the city!

For more information, please contact us at shc@pdx.edu.
