Housing + Residence Bike Parking

Portland State offers a unique variety of housing options with available bike storage.

Campus Housing Bike Parking

Bike parking and storage facilities are present in most on-campus residential buildings and available to housing residents free of charge.

Click on the image to the right for an interactive campus map of all campus buildings and facilities.
