University Budget Office

 Our Purpose 

The University Budget Office is responsible for the financial planning for the University. We provide guidance and assistance to the university community around the effective and efficient planning for and use of institutional financial resources

Our Services

Our services include annual and biennial budget planning, position management, tuition and fee book, and the preparation of financial analyses and forecasts.
