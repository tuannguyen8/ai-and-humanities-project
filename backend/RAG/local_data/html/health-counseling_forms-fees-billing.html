Release of Information (ROI): Health & Counseling

Looking to obtain your health records or send them to a new provider? 

1. Current and former Portland State University students can request their health records. 2. Filling out one Authorization ROI form covers the release of both health and mental health records. 3. Fill out one form for each provider receiving the records. 4. If you plan to self release your mental health records, an appointment needs to be made with a SHAC provider. 5. Health & Counseling Services records are only kept on file for 7 years from the last date of service. 6. You may revoke this authorization in writing at any time.

Signatures: The signature on this form must follow the Adobe Acrobat Reader - Signature Instructions. These are the only digital signatures that will be accepted.

Form Submission: You can email, fax, hand deliver (during open hours) or upload it directly to SHAC through the Patient Portal. Once in the Patient Portal, click on 'Message' on the left-hand side, and then choose 'New Message'. Then you will choose 'Upload forms and medical records'.

Student Health Insurance - Waiver Deadline Appeal

The deadline to waive the PSU Student Health Insurance Plan is midnight on Sunday of the second week of each term. This is also the last day to drop a course without financial penalty (see Academic Calendar). If a student failed to complete a waiver application before the deadline, a Missed Waiver Deadline Appeal Form can be submitted for consideration. For more information on Missed Waiver Appeal due dates and criteria, please visit the Missed Waiver Appeal information page.

Billing Information Overview

The Student Health Fee covers most Medical and Counseling Services at SHAC. However, there are some services that incur an additional cost. For students with private insurance (i.e. are not on the PSU Student Health Insurance Plan) these charges are billed to the student’s PSU account. Please note that charges can take up to a month to post onto the student account. The student can request a statement from SHAC itemizing the billable services received, and also the necessary information an insurance company needs to process a claim. The student’s insurer will decide whether the expense is covered and reimbursed.

Students with the PSU Student Health Insurance Plan may also have some charges billed to their student account such as: exclusions, dental services, and some labs. 

Requesting a Statement of Services Received

To request a statement of services received in SHAC Health Services or Counseling Services to submit to a private insurer, log-on to the Patient Portal using your ODIN login and password, and follow these steps:*

  • Verify your date of birth. 
  • Click on the box with three lines in it found in the upper lefthand corner (the "hamburger" box). 
  • Select Account Summary. 
  • You will see a history of any visit to Health or Counseling Services. There is an option to Print for each date of service. 
  • If you have any questions, difficulties, or do not see what you need, send a confidential secure message using the Patient Portal. From the main menu, select Messages. Then select the Communication Option "I have a billing question."
  • *Portland-based University of Oregon students can access the Patient Portal. If you are a Portland-based University of Oregon student who has received services at SHAC and wish to receive a statement to submit to your private insurer, visit the Patient Portal to view your Account Summary. If you’re having difficulty logging into the Patient Portal, please call 503.725.2800.
