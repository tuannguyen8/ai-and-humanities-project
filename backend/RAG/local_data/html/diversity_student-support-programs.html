Student Support & Programs

Global Diversity & Inclusion serves and empowers student populations whose success, retention, academic success, and graduation are most challenged by socio-historical factors and contemporary inequities.

Join the PSU Mentoring Program

PSU is committed to the success of all students and we encourage you to access all the incredible resources available to support your success. Our Mentoring Program matches students with a faculty or staff mentor committed to nurturing student success.
