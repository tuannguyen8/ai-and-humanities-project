Welcome to the Office of Global Engagement and Innovation, previously the Office of International Affairs.  

Our mission is to connect Portland State University, the Portland Metro area, and the world by providing a portfolio of international opportunities to students, faculty, and staff that will ensure PSU students will successfully engage in careers and the world.
