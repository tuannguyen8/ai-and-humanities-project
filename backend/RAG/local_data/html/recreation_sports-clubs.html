Sports & Clubs

Intramural Sports

Intramural Sports provide members a chance to participate in various sports leagues, tournaments, and events. It is a great opportunity to get involved on campus, make new friends, and exercise in a safe, fun environment. 

Rec Clubs

Portland State Rec Clubs is a student-led program representing different sports, games, and physical activities. Rec Clubs are organized by students and funded by student fees. We encourage participation by providing opportunities for recreation and intercollegiate competition between students of all skill levels.

Drop-In Sports & Court Schedule

Drop in on the third floor gymnasium courts to play sports, socialize, and de-stress while exercising! Court space is reserved for various drop-in sports throughout the year, including basketball, badminton, and pickleball. The Court Schedule changes each term. Check out sports equipment for free at the Equipment Check-Out Desk.
