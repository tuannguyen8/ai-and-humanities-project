Health, Science & the Earth Pathway

Contact Information:

Phone: 503-725-3822
Email: hse.advising@pdx.edu (do not use this to request appointments)
Location: 360 Fariborz Maseeh Hall

Majors and Pre-Professional Programs:

Majors: Applied Health & Fitness; Biology; Biochemistry; Chemistry; Earth Science; Environmental Science; Environmental Studies; Geography; Geology; Public Health concentrations (Community Health Promotion, Healthy Aging, Health Services Administration, Indigenous Health, Pre-Clinical Health Science, School Health Educator); Science; Speech & Hearing Sciences

Pre-professional programs: Pre-Chiropractic Medicine, Pre-Dentistry, Pre-Medicine, Pre-Naturopathic Medicine, Pre-Nursing, Pre-Occupational Therapy, Pre-Optometry, Pre-Pharmacy, Pre-Physical Therapy, Pre-Physician Assistant, Pre-Veterinary Medicine

How to meet with an HSE Advisor:

Students in this Pathway, including Exploratory, are assigned to their advisor based on the major or pre-professional program. If you would like to request a different advisor than the one assigned to you, please fill out an Advisor Change Request form. If you change your major or pre-professional program, your new advisor will be updated within a week.

Use the links below to schedule a phone, virtual or in-person appointment. You will be required to sign in with your Odin account. If you cannot access the appointment scheduler, call our front desk at (503) 725-3822. For in-person appointments, please review PSU's current Mask Policy. Please note that if you have a pre-professional program, you should meet with that advisor, regardless of your major.

Students starting at PSU in a future term: You may meet with an HSE advisor or prior to your first term, but you must participate in New Student Orientation in order to register for your first term classes. Transfer students should also check out our Transfer Guides!

*Postbaccalaureate students in Speech & Hearing Sciences may book a Zoom appointment with Jeff Conn in the SPHR Department using their online link. If these days/times don't work for you, email Jeff at connjc@pdx.edu with suggested days/times, and they may be able to set up a different appointment.

I don't know who my advisor is: Choose an option below to schedule

Find your assigned advisor in myPSU

Your assigned advisor is listed in myPSU on the Academics tab where you see your major and other academic information. Use the "Advisor Directory" link to find their scheduling page or contact them. If you do not see an advisor, use the Current Student Scheduling form on this page or call 503-725-3822.

Current student appointment scheduling

If you are a current PSU student, use the link above to be guided to the booking page for the right advisor for your program. You may schedule in-person or virtual appointments. If you need any help, please call us at 503-725-3822. You will be required to sign in with your Odin account once you reach the advisor's page.

Prospective student virtual appointments

If you have not yet attended PSU or are returning after an absence and do not remember your Odin login, click the link above to be guided to schedule with the right advisor for your program. For in-person options, please call us at 503-725-3822 or email advising@pdx.edu, so we can schedule you.
